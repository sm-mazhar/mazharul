Sky Defense: Red vs Green

Red jets hunt. Green jets flee. Aim with the mouse and left-click or hold to fire. Hitting red aircraft scores points, and different hit locations deal different damage.

Mission Briefing

Defend the green squadron against aggressive red fighters. Track fast-moving jets, manage your accuracy, and try to clear as many rounds in a row as possible before the green formation is wiped out.

How to Play

  • Move the mouse to aim the cannon and left-click or hold to fire.
  • Destroy all red jets to clear the round and advance automatically.
  • Green jets occasionally boost to escape, but if they are all eliminated the run ends.
  • Your summary appears after a loss, showing cleared rounds, time taken, and bullet usage.
